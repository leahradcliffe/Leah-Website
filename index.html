About Me

Welcome! My name is Leah Radcliffe, I’m currently a third year Information Systems major at the University of Cincinnati. I started at the University of Dayton in 2021 studying Mechanical Engineering. During my time there, I delved into the realms of molecules, structures, robotics, and coding through my coursework. I was fortunate enough to embark on a transfer to the University of Chaminade of Honolulu, where I had the privilege to engage in activities such as surfing, diving, and daily moped rides. A point of pride for me is the resilience I have cultivated through my various academic transitions. While mechanical engineering initially held promise, it became evident that it wasn’t a perfect fit. It is in my current academic pursuit at the University of Cincinnati that I have discovered my true passion. The realms of data interpretation, databases, coding, Excel, queries, and all aspects of business resonate with me deeply. Each class I attend fuels my enthusiasm, allowing me to fully immerse myself in my major and explore its myriad facets with genuine joy.

In an effort to chronicle my journey and document my growth, I have crafted this website using HTML. I am excited about the opportunities and discoveries I will make ahead.
